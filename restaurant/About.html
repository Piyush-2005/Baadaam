About Us

Welcome to BADAAM, where tradition meets taste. Nestled in the heart of Delhi, our restaurant brings the rich and diverse flavors of India to your table. From the aromatic spices of the North to the coconut-infused delicacies of the South, our menu is a culinary journey across India.


Established in 2005, BADAAM is a family-owned establishment dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that are both authentic and innovative.


Join us for a meal and experience the warmth and hospitality that India is known for. Whether you're a connoisseur of Indian cuisine or trying it for the first time, we promise to delight your taste buds and leave you with a smile.
