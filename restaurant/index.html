Our Menu

Masala Dosa

A golden, crispy South Indian crepe filled with spiced potatoes and aromatic curry leaves. Served with house-made chutneys and hearty lentil sambar.

Amritsari Kulche

Fluffy, tandoor-baked flatbread stuffed with spiced potatoes and herbs. Served with tangy tamarind chutney, cooling mint yogurt, and a hearty chole (chickpea curry).

Chhole Bhature

Fluffy, deep-fried bread paired with spiced chickpea curry. Served with tangy pickles and fresh onions.

Spring Roll

Crispy golden rolls filled with a vibrant mix of vegetables and aromatic spices. Served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Chicken Lollipops

Juicy chicken drumettes marinated in flavorful spices, deep-fried to crispy perfection. Served with a zesty dipping sauce.

Shahi Paneer

Tender paneer cubes simmered in a rich, creamy tomato and cashew gravy, infused with aromatic spices.
